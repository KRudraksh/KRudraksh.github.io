Wildlife Photography

A collection of wildlife photographs captured during my travels.

Chamaeleo Zeylanicus

Madhya Pradesh, India

The Indian chameleon (Chamaeleo zeylanicus) is native to South Asia and known for its ability to change color. These remarkable reptiles have independently moving eyes and a specialized tongue for catching prey.

Flamingo

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

Flamingos are distinctive wading birds known for their vibrant pink coloration, which comes from beta-carotene in their diet of algae, shrimp, and other crustaceans. They often stand on one leg to conserve body heat.

Southern Talapoin Monkey

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

The Southern Talapoin Monkey is one of Africa's smallest primates. These social creatures live in groups near water and are known for their agility in trees and their diverse vocal communication.

Green and Yellow Macaw

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

Macaws are colorful, long-tailed members of the parrot family. Known for their intelligence and social nature, they can mimic human speech and use their strong beaks to crack nuts and seeds in their native rainforest habitats.

Snake

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

Snakes are legless reptiles with elongated bodies and remarkable adaptations. They can detect prey through heat-sensing pits, swallow food whole, and some species possess venom used for hunting and defense.

Indian Palm Squirrel

Madhya Pradesh, India

The Indian Palm Squirrel is recognized by its distinctive stripes running down its back. These agile rodents are common in urban areas of India, where they're often considered sacred and can be seen gathering nuts and seeds.

Common Emu

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

The Common Emu is the second-largest living bird by height. Native to Australia, these flightless birds can sprint at speeds up to 30 mph. They have distinctive blue skin on their necks and are known for their booming calls.

Conure

Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain

Conures are small to medium-sized parrots known for their playful personalities and vibrant plumage. These intelligent birds are popular pets due to their social nature, vocal abilities, and long lifespans of 20+ years.
